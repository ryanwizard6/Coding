For President: Fisher Gueterman

Why Fisher?

  • Environmental Strategy: He believes that the environment needs to be cleaner and that we need to all use more cleaner energy.
  • Business Strategy: He has a strong belief that we should be able to aid the small businesses so that the bigger companies don't take all the spotlight and that the small businesses also get some recognition.
  • Global Relationships and Immigration: He wants to make good relationships with all of our countries and make our immigration system more efficient and more affective at getting people into the country.
  • Domestic Policy: Fisher thinks that we need to reduce the debt by reducing spending to areas that already have enough money, and also spend on what the people want and need.
  • Foreign Policy: He believes that we need to increase our bonds with other countries and to limit trade to countries that aren't trustworthy.

Why Camden?

He thinks that we should also fund ICE and border patrol to help get rid of illegal immigrants but we should however encourage the legal process to come to the countryt and provide outreach to struggling countries.
  • Domestic Policy: He believes that we should provide what the people ened by making legislation ban recreational drugs such as nicotine and smoking and that we should be providing education on guns to the people so we can be trained to handle one safely.
  • Foreign Policy: He wants a foreign policy prioritizing national interests by balancing state interaction, economic tools, and military strength, while collaborating with Congress on treaties and funding, and addressing global issues like human rights, trade, and security challenges.
